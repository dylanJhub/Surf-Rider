🏄 Surf Rider
Overview
Surf Rider is an action-packed arcade game where you control a daring surfer riding down a massive wave. Navigate treacherous waters by dodging dangerous sea creatures while racking up points and competing for the highest score!

🎮 Gameplay
Control your surfer as they ride an endless wave down the ocean. The challenge? Avoid colliding with sea animals that populate the waters around you. Every successful dodge increases your score, and staying alive as long as possible is the ultimate goal.

🐋 Sea Creatures
Watch out for these ocean inhabitants:

Sharks 🦈 - Dangerous predators
Dolphins 🐬 - Playful but hazardous
Seals 🦭 - Unexpected obstacles
⭐ Features
Dynamic Difficulty - The longer you survive, the faster enemies move! Every 10 seconds, all sea animals increase their speed.
Points System - Dodge enemies to earn points, and gain bonus points every 10 seconds just for surviving.
Lives System - Start with 3 lives. Lose one each time you hit a sea animal. Game ends when all lives are lost.
Score Tracking - Track your best scores and compete to beat your personal record.
🎯 Scoring
Dodge Bonus - Gain points each time you successfully avoid a sea animal
Survival Bonus - Earn 100 points every 10 seconds for staying alive
Increasing Difficulty - Animals get faster every 10 seconds, making dodging more challenging and rewarding
💔 Lives & Game Over
You start with 3 lives
Colliding with any sea animal costs you 1 life
When you lose all 3 lives, the game ends
Your final score is recorded and compared with your previous best
🏆 Objective
The goal is simple but challenging: Survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score possible! Beat your previous score and climb the leaderboard.

🎮 How to Play
Start the game and take control of your surfer
Navigate left and right to avoid incoming sea animals
Stay focused as the game speeds up every 10 seconds
Rack up points by dodging obstacles and surviving
Try to beat your personal best score!
Good luck, surfer! 🌊
